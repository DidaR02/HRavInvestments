Broad Based BEE

H-Rav Investments is 100% Black Owned. We are committed to the principles and best practices of Economic Empowerment, Employment Equity and Skills Development. We remain fully committed to the objectives of the South African Government to extend broad economic participation through empowerment and to transform and diversify ownership within the domestic economy. Our Key Objective is to create employment opportunities for previously disadvantaged individuals. Our team consists of highly skilled black and female professionals.
